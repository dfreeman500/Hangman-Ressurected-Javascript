Hangman Resurrected

I am the world's greatest guessor of words. The goal is to guess any English word you can think of. The bigger the word, the better I am. Tell me how many letters your word has in the box above.

Info:

Welcome to my simple hangman game! Most internet hangman games have the user guess the computer's word. But in this game "I" will try to guess your word. The goal is to guess any and all English words in as few guesses as possible ... but that's pretty difficult. Think of a word and enter how long the word is in the box above. I will then try to guess your word by choosing the most likely letters from the set of words I have in my lists. Along the way, you'll see what words the list are candidates and which are eliminated. You can click on some of the words to get a definition.

You can use this program to play other online hangman games.

As I guess your word, I will put candidate words in this box. Click them to find out the definitions.
